RIFTI has two Film Schools in Hyderabad, one at Dilsukhnagar and the other at Srinagar Colony, the campus is designed with tranquil surroundings for a unique study experience.

RIFTI Film School in Dilsukhnagar

The Film School in Dilsukhnagar is Surrounded by education institutes and green spaces, RIFTI Dilsukhnagar Campus is located in the heart of the education hub in Dilsukhnagar,Hyderabad.

It has first-rate teaching and student library, as well as student accommodation.

RIFTI Film School in Srinagar Colony

The Film School in Srinagar Colony in the neighborhood has a lively atmosphere and is at the center of Film Business making it the ideal place to study.

The campus is surrounded by locations for film shoots, as well as the KBR Park and a selection of malls and restaurants.

Many sites around the campus have been used as sets for many telugu films.

  • Bus Stop

Facilities

The campus is renowned for its excellent teaching and shooting films. We have recently invested into film equipment and two new floors.
